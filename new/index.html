ABOUT US

Loth Robotics was established in March 2019 by Sakıp Sabancı Anatolian High School students as an FRC team. In 2021, we decided to grow our team to become our school's robotics club. Today there are 46 members, 2 mentors and an administrative team consisting of a leader, 4 vice leaders and 2 general coordinators in our team.
