ABOUT US

FRC team 8182 was established in March 2019 by Sakıp Sabancı Anatolian High School students. The team started with 5 students and 2 mentors. In September, we came together with İSOV Dinçkök Vocational High School and established a team of 18 students and 5 mentors. While 2 of our mentors are responsible for events and organizations, the others are responsible for technical work.
